Available positions

PhDs and Postdocs: My research group is always looking for excellent PhD students and post-docs focusing on network security, distributed ledger technology, autonomous systems and security with a focus on Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and digital twins. Make a PhD enquiry on the themes mentioned above or even with your ideas and funding relevant to secure computing or network security.
